Welcome to Re-Cover: Finding the Lost Art of Creative Writing's Online Exhibit! In this website, you will learn about the presence of creative writing in different aspects of media and entertainment; some might be obvious, but others will be unexpected!

We want to show young people how common the use of creative writing is in their lives - from pocketbooks all the way to the games you play! In the process, maybe we can even inspire a few young minds to dabble into the art of writing creative fiction.
