Trivias:

What is the oldest genre of Creative Writing?

The oldest genre is poetry. The term poetry originates from the Latin word for “poet,” which means “maker” or “author.” The original term poiḗtria refers to all creative writing. So, in the beginning, all creative writing was poetry.

Who was the first Creative Writer?

The world's first known author is widely considered to be Enheduanna, a woman who lived in the 23rd century BCE in ancient Mesopotamia (approximately 2285-2250 BCE).
